Dolce Mochaccino

A delightful infusion of espresso, chocolate, and sweet milk.

Ingredients

50 mL - espresso

50 mL - whole milk

50 mL - milk for dry microfoam

2 g - dark cacao

To your taste - sweetened condensed milk

Chef It

1. Pour steamed milk into 1/3 of cup.

2. Mix in swetened condensed milk.

3. Froth a separate milk, to dry consistency, and top.

4. Bring water to just below boiling.

5. Place filter over cup.

6. Pour water into filter just enough to cover beans and cacao.

7. Pour more water in a circular motion.

8. Care to pour coffee through foam and layer over milk.

9. Sprinkle espresso grains and shaved cacao nibs over foam.

-

Enjoy
